IT-Related Ethics

In the fast-evolving world of technology, ethics are essential. Here are some key guidelines:

  • Developers should write secure and tested code, respect user privacy and report and fix bugs.
  • When using a open source they have a responsibilty of giving proper credit.
  • AI and machine learning models should be explainable and transparent.
  • IT professionals should not abuse their access to thier companys data.
